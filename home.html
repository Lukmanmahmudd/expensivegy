About Kanawa Taxi Service (KSIP)

Kanawa Taxi Service (KSIP) was founded with the mission to redefine the standards of public transportation in Kano State. Spearheaded by the former governor Abdullahi Umar Ganduje, KSIP is dedicated to offering unparalleled taxi services that prioritize safety, efficiency, and customer satisfaction. Our fleet is comprised of state-of-the-art vehicles that are meticulously maintained to ensure a smooth and comfortable journey for all passengers. Whether you're traveling within the city or to its outskirts, KSIP is committed to providing a reliable and hassle-free transportation experience.

Our Vision and Commitment

At Kanawa Taxi Service (KSIP), our vision extends beyond merely providing transportation. We seek to build a network that connects people across Kano State with ease and efficiency. Founded under the guidance of Abdullahi Umar Ganduje, KSIP is a testament to his commitment to improving the lives of Kano residents through innovative solutions. Our taxis are equipped with the latest technology to ensure punctuality, safety, and the highest standards of service. KSIP is not just about getting you from point A to point B; it's about creating a travel experience that is seamless, dependable, and tailored to your needs.
